About the Forum

Bringing Together
Diverse Perspectives

The Redefining Learning Forum is an intimate, expert-led roundtable series where a diverse group of EdTech stakeholders come together to tackle the most pressing challenges and opportunities in education.

Each session is carefully curated to foster meaningful dialogue, generate actionable insights, and build lasting connections across the EdTech ecosystem.
