Why It Exists

Coaches Change Everything

Coaches are fundamental to startup development. They are a pivotal part in guiding founders to reach their full potential.

Unlike mentoring, coaching doesn't tell you what to do. Instead, it fosters independent thinking — and it's this mindset shift that drives transformative growth in founders.

The GSCA was created to recognise and celebrate the work coaches do behind the scenes. Their efforts deserve a spotlight.

The Mission

Transform Education Through Technology

Whether transitioning from mentoring, or coming from a completely different background with transferable skills — the GSCA is for you.

We look for coaches with versatile experience across sectors. The mission: use coaching to improve access, equity, and quality of education globally.

Are you an impactful coach passionate about startup development? The GSCA is your stage.

Who Qualifies

Who Should Apply?

The GSCA welcomes coaches from all backgrounds. If you are passionate about startup development and driving impact in EdTech, this programme is for you.

🎯

Active Startup Coaches

Coaches currently working in startup development who want their impact recognised on a global stage.

🔄

Transitioning from Mentoring

Professionals moving from a mentoring role to a coaching methodology, developing independent thinking in founders.

🌐

Cross-Sector Professionals

Coaches with versatile experience across industries — tech, finance, education — with skills transferable to EdTech startups.

💡

Career Changers

Individuals from non-coaching backgrounds with demonstrable transferable skills relevant to startup development.

🚀

EdTech Enthusiasts

Individuals deeply passionate about the development of education technology startups and driving societal impact.

📊

Business-Background Coaches

Coaches with EdTech and business backgrounds, recognised coaching qualifications, and proven startup experience.

How It Works

24-Week Programme Structure

A structured journey from application to award ceremony — three phases designed to surface the world's most impactful startup coaches.

1

Screening

Weeks 1 – 2

After reviewing all global applications, 20 coaches are selected based on coaching qualifications, startup coaching experience, EdTech & business background, and core coaching competencies.

🧑‍💼 20 coaches selected from global pool
2

Diagnosis

Weeks 3 – 6

Each coach is matched with a pre-selected startup. Through intensive diagnosis, the coach outlines key objectives for the acceleration phase tailored to the startup's unique mission.

🔍 4 weeks of intensive startup diagnosis
3

Acceleration

Weeks 7 – 24

Each coach conducts weekly 90-minute coaching sessions with their startup and submits a coaching report after every session over 20 weeks.

⏱ 90 min/week · 20 sessions total
