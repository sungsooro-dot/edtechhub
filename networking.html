About

Breaking Down Silos Across the Ecosystem

EdTech HUB runs a growing number of programmes — from the Global Startup Coach Award to the Redefining Learning Forum. Cross-Cohort Networking is the initiative that connects participants across all of these, creating a unified community that is greater than the sum of its parts.

Whether you're a programme alumnus, an active cohort member, or a community partner — Cross-Cohort Networking gives you structured access to the people and opportunities across the entire EdTech HUB ecosystem.

We believe the best collaborations happen across disciplines, roles, and geographies. This initiative creates the conditions for exactly that.

Who This Is For
🎓 Programme participants & alumni
🚀 EdTech founders & startup teams
💰 Investors & mentors in our network
🎓 Educators & learning designers
🌐 Partnership organisation members
🏛️ Policy & research stakeholders
Process

How Cross-Cohort Networking Works

01
Join or Complete a Programme
Participation in any EdTech HUB initiative — events, cohorts, or community programmes — gives you access to the cross-cohort network.
02
Get Matched & Connected
We facilitate structured introductions across cohorts based on your goals, interests, and the value you can offer to others in the network.
03
Build & Collaborate
Join cross-cohort sessions, meetups, and online spaces designed to turn introductions into lasting professional relationships and collaborations.
Benefits

What You Get

🤝
Structured Introductions
Warm introductions to people across the EdTech HUB ecosystem who are relevant to your specific goals — founders, investors, educators, or partners.
🌍
Global Community Access
Connect with ecosystem members across London, Singapore, Seoul, Sydney, and online — a truly international EdTech network.
💡
Cross-Discipline Insights
Gain perspectives from different roles — investors talking with educators, founders collaborating with policymakers — driving richer innovation.
🔗
Lasting Relationships
Go beyond the event or programme. Cross-Cohort Networking is designed to create connections that outlast individual initiatives.
