About Partnership Events

Driving Innovation
Through Collaboration

Beyond our monthly EdTech Together meetups, we organise collaborative events with some of the key players in the EdTech scene — bringing together organisations, institutions, and individuals who share a commitment to advancing education through technology.

Our Partnership Events are designed to spark meaningful conversations, forge lasting relationships, and create opportunities that no single organisation could achieve alone.

We believe in the power of community and collaboration. If you're interested in partnering with us to host or co-create an event, we'd love to hear from you.
