Our Mission

Why EdTech HUB Exists

The global EdTech sector is growing fast — but it remains fragmented. Startups struggle to find the right investors. Educators can't easily discover what's working. Policymakers are disconnected from innovation at the ground level.

EdTech HUB was founded to fix that. We exist to be the connective layer of the EdTech ecosystem — a trusted, active community where the people shaping the future of education can find each other, share knowledge, and build together.

With operations running across London, Singapore, Seoul, Sydney, and online, we host monthly events, curate the latest news, and run programmes that create real opportunities across the ecosystem.

"Together, not against each other — that's how we make EdTech work for everyone."

Community First
We build genuine relationships, not just networks. Every event, programme, and initiative is designed to create lasting connections.
Global, Local
Operating across London, Singapore, Seoul, Sydney, and online — we connect local ecosystems to the global stage.
Evidence-Driven
We champion EdTech that works. Our news, events, and programmes focus on real outcomes and learning evidence, not just hype.
Open & Inclusive
Whether you're a founder, a teacher, an investor, or a student — there's a place for you in the EdTech HUB community.
